Unit 1: Subject-Verb Agreement

The subject and verb must agree in number. Singular subjects take singular verbs, and plural subjects take plural verbs.

❌ Incorrect: Everyone have completed the quiz.
can use: Everyone has completed the quiz.

Unit 2: Active vs. Passive Voice

Use active voice when the subject performs the action. It makes your sentences stronger, direct, and easier to read.

ℹ️ Active: The developer wrote the clean code.
ℹ️ Passive: The clean code was written by the developer.
