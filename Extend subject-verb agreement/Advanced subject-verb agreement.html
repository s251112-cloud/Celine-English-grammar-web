Subject-Verb Agreement

Unit 10: Advanced Style Guide Rules 🐾

1. Data: Singular or Plural?

Style guides allow data to be singular or plural depending on your focus:

Singular Example: The data is showing that the kittens are healthy.
Plural Example: These data are clear; the female scientists published their results.

2. None: Is or Are?

"None" changes based on whether you mean "not one" or are talking about a plural group:

  • Singular: None of the milk is left for the puppy.
  • Plural: None of the girls are going to the park today.

3. Pronoun Forms

Subject Pronoun Object Pronoun Example
She Her She told me that GitHub belongs to her.
They Them The girls won, so the teacher praised them.
